Grind Coffee - IOS APP

Category : UX - UI / IOS App
Completion : Jan 2019
Sector : E-commerce

COFFEE SHOP CHALLENGE

Working on a coffee ordering app. The design challenge was that users could have coffee staff deliver the coffee to any location or set a time to go pick up the coffee.

Research

The research showed a common problem: people often get late to work due to crowds in coffee shops.

  • - More than 80% said they can not start a day withou a coffee.
  • - 85% said they don't have time enought for daily tasks
  • - 63% said they like custom coffee

Sitemap

The basis of any usable and successful navigation flow is the prior determination of the information architecture. The information must be well organized, identifying all possible access points (for example: user sells products - different users purchase those products, human resources, dashboards, etc.) so that we visualize a consistent structure which will help drawing user flows.

Wireframes

Wireframing means creating the skeleton of the product. It is an easy and concise sketch that will help us improve the navigability. This process has a minimum cost of resources, is fast and allows us to test and iterate to make all the necessary adaptations.

FROM UX TO UI

Visual

After organizing the information architecture and creating user flows I designed a moodboard to generate a visual brainstorm that helped me determine shape, color, tone and illustrations as a guide. Next step: the Style Guide.

The Style Guide has components; most of them are atoms and molecules. These components will give rise to the Atomic Design that is composed of five stages: atoms, molecules, organisms, templates and pages. Atomic design is not a linear process, but rather a mental model to help us think of our user interfaces as both a cohesive whole and a collection of parts at the same time. Each of the five stages plays a key role in the hierarchy of our interface design systems.

Result

The final product is a coffee ordering App by which users can keep the preferences active and placing orders easily and fast!.
