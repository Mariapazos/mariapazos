GOING

Category : Web & App
Completion : July 2019
Sector : UI

For those who are looking for a different way of travel planning. "Search locations based on your favorite movies, series or singers".

The development consists of a web to organize trips breaking with the schemes of the habitual search. Various statistics (presented in the Experience Fighters 2019 event) affirm that 80% of users who travel avoid the most demanded destinations and prefer a personalized and unique organization.

PRODUCT ADVANTAGES AND FEATURES

Self Determination Theory

  • - Control: location search based on favorite movies, series or singers. Can also design own roads.
  • - Skills:different levels of traveler (Beginner, medium, expert and Goinner). As the user travels, more points will be obtained for organizational advantages and discounts.
  • - Connectivity: The opinions of other users can be known and contrasted and the ratings of the destinations can be checked.

Once the route is defined, it will only be necessary to prepare the luggage! Once the user left home will be able to follow the route with the App that will serve as gps in addition to being notified with specific information about the destination in which user is at that moment. As user progresses, the App will notify the percentage left to finish the route.
