imovi - IOS APP

Category : UI Design / IOS Apps
Client : Own
Completion : Feb 2019
Sector : Aumented reality

imovi

The objective of imovi is to improve the user experience in relation to the cinematographic world, including the purchase of tickets, selection of seats, recommendations, etc.

One of the most interesting points of the product focuses on the waste of user time since he arrives at the cinema until the film begins; how to face it? how to create a user experience that makes him return to our facilities? Making him share in his own experience! Let's use augmented reality, in this way once the user arrives at the cinema and check the App, will discover countless characters that seem to have escaped from the movie!

After organizing the information architecture, I designed a moodboard to generate a visual brainstorming that helped me determine shape, color, tone and illustrations as a guide. Including an onboarding will help the user to better understand all the features of the product.

Discover. The user will find a world of films categorized to facilitate their search as well as recommended lists generated based on their tastes. Book. The user can book the seats he likes the most. Enjoy. The superheroes have escaped from movies, can you find them around you?
