AnimaLook - APP

Category : UX - UI / Apps
Client : Own
Completion : Dec 2018
Sector : Pet adoption

NEXT STEPS ANIMALOOK WEB
The product is a mobile application with "My pet" section where the clinical information of it is located. The clinical record will be included in a device inside the collar of the pet so that the user can synchronize the device with the App and can perform functions such as activating alerts of future vaccination dates in addition to consulting de-worming, diseases or any type of information related to health. In this way vets could modify the timely information easily after appointments with users and pets in clinics.

In this way, once the user has interacted with Animalook Web platform for the first time, we will encourage you to continue being part of the product with this App Version and thus have a follow-up of the clinical history of the adpted pet.
